Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal data.

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dar Mama Vacation Homes Rental Co. L.L.C ("Dar Mama", "we", "us") collects, uses and protects personal data when you use our website dar-mama.com. We are committed to handling your data lawfully and transparently, in line with the UAE Personal Data Protection Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021) and, where it applies, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Who we are

Dar Mama is a short-term rental and holiday-home management company based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates (DET-licensed holiday homes). For any privacy question, contact us at contact@dar-mama.com.

Data we collect

  • Information you give us: when you submit the revenue-estimate form we collect your name, email address, property area/building, number of bedrooms and any message you add.
  • Contact you initiate: if you reach us by WhatsApp, phone or email, we receive the details you choose to share.
  • Usage and cookies: with your consent, we use analytics cookies to understand how the site is used (for example pages viewed and general location). We do not use non-essential cookies until you accept them.

How we use your data

  • To reply to your enquiry and prepare a revenue estimate for your property.
  • To provide and improve our services and this website.
  • To measure and improve our marketing, where you have consented to analytics.

Legal basis

We process form data on the basis of your request and our legitimate interest in responding to prospective clients. Analytics and advertising cookies are processed only on the basis of your consent, which you can withdraw at any time by clearing cookies in your browser.

Cookies and analytics

On first visit we show a cookie banner. If you accept, we may load Google Analytics 4 (audience measurement) and the Meta (Facebook) Pixel (advertising measurement). If you decline, these are not loaded. Essential functionality (such as remembering your language choice) does not require consent.

Sharing with third parties

We use trusted service providers who process data on our behalf, including our form/email provider, analytics providers (Google, Meta) where you have consented, and WhatsApp if you choose to message us. We do not sell your personal data. Some providers may process data outside the UAE; where required we rely on appropriate safeguards.

Data retention

We keep enquiry data only as long as needed to respond and, if you become a client, for the duration of our relationship and any legal retention period. Otherwise we delete or anonymise it when it is no longer needed.

Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, object to certain processing, or withdraw consent. To exercise any right, email contact@dar-mama.com and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.

This document is provided for transparency and general information. It is not legal advice; for a binding policy tailored to your obligations, please have it reviewed by a qualified legal professional.
