Our story

A Dubai host's care, with a manager's discipline

Dar Mama was founded on a simple belief: short-term rental owners deserve both peace of mind and the truth. Dubai's market is competitive and fast-moving, and too many owners hand over their keys only to receive vague updates and opaque statements.

We built Dar Mama to be different — combining warm, attentive hospitality with the rigour of hotel-grade revenue management, and backing it with something almost no competitor offers: a real-time platform where you can see exactly how your property is performing, any time.

The founders

Two operators, one standard of care

Dar Mama isn't a faceless agency. It's two people who have spent their careers in hospitality and decided to do it properly — for owners like you.

I

Ismail

Co-founder · Commercial, Marketing & Revenue Management

Ismail spent 15 years inside the world's most demanding hospitality groups — Marriott, Accor and Disney — leading commercial, marketing and revenue management teams. There he learned how great hotels turn data into the right price at the right moment, and what true service really feels like. He founded Dar Mama to bring that same discipline to individual owners: every apartment priced and run like a hotel room, every decision backed by numbers you can actually see.

D

Dounia

Co-founder · Customer Success & Operations

Dounia has called Dubai home for 12 years, building her career in customer success and on-the-ground operations. She has overseen countless renovation and upgrade projects — turning tired apartments into homes guests rave about and owners are proud of. Warm with guests and relentless on detail, she makes sure every Dar Mama property looks and feels its best, stay after stay.
